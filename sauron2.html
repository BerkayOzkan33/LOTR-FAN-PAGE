There Is No Life In The Void, Only Death...

Renowned student of the Vala Aulë, and most trusted lieutenant of Morgoth in the First Age. After Morgoth's ousting by the Valar, Sauron became the second Dark Lord and sought to conquer Middle-earth through guileful trickery, with the Rings of Power or with overwhelming legions. At the end of the Second Age, Sauron caused the downfall of Númenor, and was then defeated in Mordor in the War of the Last Alliance of Elves and Men united under Gil-galad and Elendil. In the final battle, Isildur managed to cut the One Ring from him, dismantling his corporeal form and ridding much of his power. After centuries lying dormant, rebuilding strength and stirring awake evil things, Sauron returned to power late in the Third Age.
