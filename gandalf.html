"He wore a tall pointed blue hat, a long grey cloak, and a silver scarf. He had a long white beard and bushy eyebrows that stuck out beyond the brim of his hat."

Gandalf was one of the five Istari sent to Middle-earth by the Valar in the Third Age. In Valinor he was known as Olórin. Gandalf was instrumental in bringing about the demise of Sauron in T.A. 3019, chiefly by encouraging others and dispensing his wisdom at pivotal times. He was originally robed in grey, and second to Saruman in the Order of Wizards. After his fall in Moria, Gandalf returned to Middle-earth as head of the Order, robed in white.

Originally called Olórin. He was accounted as the wisest of the Maiar.
